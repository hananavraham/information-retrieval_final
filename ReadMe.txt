

My Google :
------------


AND = "&" ->    word & word
OR = "|"  ->    word | word
NOT = "!" ->	(word & word) ! word , (word | word) ! word




extra:

1. long string : "and his wife" (only 3 words)

2. Wild Character/Joker : "voi" -> got result : voice, void

3. Near : "moses & israel [1,8]" -> the distance between word1 to word 2 is 1 to 8 words.
